List of User Agreements

    Terms of Service (ToS): A legal agreement between the service provider and the user, outlining the rules and guidelines for using the service. End User License Agreement (EULA): A contract between the software application's licensor and the purchaser, granting the right to use the software. Privacy Policy: A statement that explains how an organization collects, handles, and processes users' personal data. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP): Defines what actions are and are not allowed when using a service, often pertaining to appropriate behavior, content, or usage. Service Level Agreement (SLA): An agreement between a service provider and a customer that defines the level of service expected from the service provider. Return and Refund Policy: Outlines the conditions under which a customer can return a purchased item and receive a refund. Affiliate Agreement: An agreement between a company and its affiliates that sets out the terms and conditions for the partnership. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): A legal contract that outlines confidential material, knowledge, or information that the parties wish to share with one another for certain purposes but restrict access to or by third parties. Community Guidelines: Rules that outline the acceptable behaviors and content standards for users in an online community. Copyright Policy: Outlines the rules and regulations related to the use of copyrighted material on a platform or service. Code of Conduct: A set of rules outlining the social norms and rules and responsibilities of, or proper practices for, an individual, party, or organization.
